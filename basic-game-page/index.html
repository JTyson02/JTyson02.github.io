Axis and Allies: World at War

General information

  • Published by Avalon Hill Games, Inc
  • Designed by Larry Harris Jr
  • Released in 2008
  • Genre: Strategy, Wargame
  • Players: 2-6

Game Description

Axis and Allies is a 2-6 player board Game built around strategy, luck, and decisiveness. Each player
can take the role of a major power of WW2, from America, Great Britain, or Russia to Imperial Japan, Facist Italy, and Nazi Germany.
players take turns buying troops, attacking, researching, and finally placing the purchased troops where they're needed most.
