Welcome to Not Quite So Dark Dungeon. This is a simple text-based adventure that includes auto-battling features. Navigate through the world using the buttons available, name your character (and don't forget to save them frequently!), and explore the wilderness to earn money. Sleep in the tavern to heal and upgrade your stats in the general store! Fighting enemies is a simple auto battler, but beware that they hit back! Down at the bottom of the screen is the Log, where you can find flavortext and interactions. Good luck future Hero!
